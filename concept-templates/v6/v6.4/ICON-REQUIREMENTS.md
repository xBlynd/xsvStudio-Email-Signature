# Icon Requirements for v6.4

## Icons Needed (9 files)

### Contact Icons (14x14px)
1. **email.png** - Envelope icon, violet (#5c5edc)
2. **phone.png** - Phone icon, violet (#5c5edc)
3. **globe.png** - Globe icon, violet (#5c5edc)

### Social Icons (18x18px)
4. **verification.png** - Checkmark in circle, violet (#5c5edc)
5. **globe-social.png** - Globe icon, violet (#5c5edc) at 80% opacity
6. **linkedin.png** - LinkedIn logo, official blue (#0A66C2)
7. **youtube.png** - YouTube logo, official red (#FF0000)
8. **github.png** - GitHub logo, black (#181717)
9. **instagram.png** - Instagram logo, gradient (use official colors)

---

## Export Settings

- **Format:** PNG with transparency
- **Resolution:** 2x for retina (export at 28x28 for contact, 36x36 for social, display at half size)
- **Background:** Transparent
- **Style:** Flat, simple (Font Awesome or similar)

---

## Where to Get Icons

Use one of these sources:

1. **Simple Icons** (https://simpleicons.org/)
   - LinkedIn, YouTube, GitHub, Instagram official logos
   - Export as SVG, convert to PNG

2. **Font Awesome** (https://fontawesome.com/)
   - Envelope, phone, globe, check-circle
   - Download icon, color in Figma/Illustrator, export PNG

3. **Iconify** (https://icon-sets.iconify.design/)
   - Search for each icon
   - Download SVG, convert to PNG with correct colors
